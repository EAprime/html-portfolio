The 3 Best Movie Franchises According to Eduardo

My Top 3 Movie Franchises

The James Bond Movie Franchise

I grew up watching Pierce Brosnan play a smooth, suave and cool James Bond. Since then I have watched all 25 films of the British spy.

Star Wars

I love the idea of using the force and having a battle between good and evil with swords that light up in different colors

The Harry Potter films

Great movies that follow the characters battling against evil in the magical world from kids to young adults.
